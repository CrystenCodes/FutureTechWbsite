Rich Content in the Online Environment & User Experience

5th month: Rich Content in the Online Environment & User Experience

Key Ideas:

• Rich content refers to online content that goes beyond plain text — such as videos, animations, interactive features, audio, and infographics — designed to make the experience more engaging, informative, and user-centric.

• Rich content encourages immediate interaction, improved usability, and better emotional and cognitive engagement, thereby enhancing the overall user experience.

• Active user participation (e.g., commenting, sharing, creating content) is an indication of effective rich media integration.
