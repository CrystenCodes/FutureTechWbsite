Team Structure and Dynamics for ICT Content

A clear team structure helps create ICT content efficiently. Key roles include:

Leader/Project Manager: Guides the team, sets goals, tracks progress, and resolves issues.

Researchers: Gather accurate, reliable information and summarize it clearly.

Designers/Developers: Turn research into user-friendly content, visuals, or interactive tools.

Editors/QA: Check for accuracy, clarity, consistency, and technical functionality.

Team Dynamics:

Communicate clearly using agreed channels.

Respect deadlines and each member’s contributions.

Collaborate on problem-solving and support each other when needed.

This setup ensures smooth teamwork and high-quality ICT content.
