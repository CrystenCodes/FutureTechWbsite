Artificial Intelligence In Computers

AI (Artificial Intelligence) refers to computer systems that are able to do complex tasks only humans could do (historically)

There are many types of AI including:

Narrow: Designed for specific tasks but lacks generalization

Like Siri or Alexa

General: Designed to perform any intellectual task a human can do

Still a theoretical concept

Superintelligent: Surpasses the human mind (theory)

Each AI has a function built for specific tasks.

ChatGPT: generates images, information, and code

Wix: generates website designs

Meta AI: specializes in chatting
