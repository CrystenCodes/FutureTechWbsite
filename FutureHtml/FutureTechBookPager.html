Web 2.0 and Web 3.0

This lesson explores the evolution of the internet from Web 1.0 (static websites) to Web 2.0 (interactive and social platforms) and Web 3.0 (smart, decentralized, and secure web technologies). Students will understand how the web developed, how users interact with modern platforms, and how future internet systems may work.

Artificial Intelligence

This topic introduces Artificial Intelligence (AI), including how machines simulate human intelligence. It covers machine learning, automation, real-world AI applications (like chatbots and smart assistants), and how AI impacts industries, education, and everyday life.

Convergent and Assistive Media

Learn how different types of media (text, audio, video, and digital platforms) merge together in modern communication. This lesson also explores assistive technologies that help individuals with disabilities access information and communicate more effectively using digital tools.

ICT in the Context of Global Communication

This lesson examines how Information and Communication Technology (ICT) connects people worldwide. It discusses digital communication tools, social media, online collaboration, cybersecurity, and how technology influences global business, culture, and education.
