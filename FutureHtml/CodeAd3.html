Logic in coding

Logic in Coding Logic in coding is the process of applying structured and critical thinking to solve problems through programming.

It involves using conditions, Boolean operators (such as AND, OR, and NOT), and step-by-step reasoning to create algorithms that produce accurate outputs. Logical thinking helps programmers determine how a program should behave under different situations.

and ensures that instructions are clear, efficient, and free from errors. Strong coding logic is essential for building reliable software and solving complex computational problems.
