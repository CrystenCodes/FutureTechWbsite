assistive media

assistive media is a type of media that is designed to help people with disabilities or special needs to access information and communication.

it can include a wide range of technologies and tools, such as screen readers, voice recognition software, and alternative input devices. assistive media can help people with disabilities to participate in the digital world and access information and communication that they might not otherwise be able to access. it can also help to improve the quality of life for people with disabilities by providing them with greater independence and access to resources.
