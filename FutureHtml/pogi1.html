Hardware:

Hardware denotes the physical components of a computer system. This includes internal components such as CPU, motherboard, memory, storage, and external peripherals like keyboard, mouse, monitor, printer, etc. Hardware works with software to perform computing tasks, process data, and produce output.

Types of Hardware:

1. Input Devices – Devices for users to enter data. Example: Keyboard, mouse, scanner.

2. Output Devices – Devices to display results or information. Example: Monitor, printer, speakers.
