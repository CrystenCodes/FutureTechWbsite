Fourth Week: the current state of ICT technologies

ICT in Global Communication

ICT has become the main method of communication in today's society.

It helps in getting information, education, and accessing services from businesses, hospitals, etc.

Context of Communication in ICT:

Education: Using computers to type reports, follow course instructions, and store student/school information.

Telecommunication: Sending pictures, letters, and messages through digital systems.

Commerce: Shops and supermarkets use ICT to calculate bills and improve efficiency.

Sports and Recreation: Used to track daily sports activities and performance.

Arts and Design: Editing videos, photos, and music, which are shared online.

Media and Visual Arts: Used for animation, architecture, and digital design.

ICT has many uses in our society and will continue to improve in the coming years.
